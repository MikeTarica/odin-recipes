Method

  1. Preheat oven to 230°C / 210°C fan-forced. Line a baking tray or pizza tray with baking paper.

    2. Please configure the Recipe Tip in the dialog.

  2. Place pizza base on lined tray. Spread with the pizza sauce. Scatter over the Perfect Pizza cheese. Top with pepperoni slices.

    3. Please configure the Recipe Tip in the dialog.

  3. Bake for 15 minutes or until cheese is melted and golden and base is crisp. Serve scattered with basil leaves if you like.
